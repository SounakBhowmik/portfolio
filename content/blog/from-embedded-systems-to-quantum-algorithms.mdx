## Engineering instincts still matter

Embedded work taught me to value constraints: memory, latency, power, reliability. Quantum research has different constraints, but the same discipline helps.

## A shift in abstraction

Instead of debugging timing loops, I now think about unitary evolution and noise channels. The questions changed; the curiosity did not.

## Looking forward

I am interested in building bridges: practical experiments that connect sensing hardware, learning systems, and quantum information theory.
